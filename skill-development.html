How It Works

From application to livelihood — a clear 4-step journey.

1

Register

Apply online or at your nearest FWF community centre with basic ID proof.

2

Training

Attend structured 30–90 day hands-on training at our local skill centre.

3

Certificate

Receive a nationally recognised FWF skill certificate upon completion.

4

Livelihood

Get market linkages, microloan referral, or direct employment support.
